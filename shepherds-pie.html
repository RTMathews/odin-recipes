Shepherd's Pie

A beef and vegetable pie topped with mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

Meat Filling

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 2 teaspoons dried parsley leaves
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 garlic cloves -minced
  • 2 tablespoons all purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • 1/2 cup frozen carrots
  • 1/2 cup frozen corn kernels

Potato Topping

  • 2 lb. russet potatoes
  • 8 tablespoons or 1 stick butter
  • 1/3 cup cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese

Steps

Meat Filling

  1. Add oil to large skillet place on medium-high heat 2 minutes. Add onions, cook 5 minutes.
  2. Add ground beef to skillet. Add parsley, rosemary, salt, and pepper. Cook 6 to 8 minutes, until meat is browned
  3. Add Worcestershire sauce and garlic. Cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add flour and tomato paste, stir.
  5. Add broth, frozen peas, frozen carrots, and frozen corn. Bring liquid to boil then reduce to simmer.
  6. Set meat mixture aside. Preheat oven 400 degrees F.

    7. Potato Topping

  7. Place potatoes in large pot. Cover with water and bring to boil. Cook until fork tender, 10 to 15 minutes.
  8. Drain patatoes in a colander. Return them to the hot pot.
  9. Add butter, cream, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mash potatoes and stir until all ingredients are mixed together.
  10. Add parmesan cheese. Stir until well mixed.

    11. Assemble Casserole

  11. Pour meat mixture into 9x9 inch baking dish. Spread out into even layer. Spoon mashed potatoes on top of meat and spread into an even layer.
  12. Place on rimmed baking sheet. Bake uncovered for 25-30 minutes. Cool for 15 minutes before serving.
